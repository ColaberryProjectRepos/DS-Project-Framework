Phase 2: Doing the project - Data Science Projects

This document is there to help you structure your data science project. The flow of this guide will help you craft a data science project quickly and pay attention to some of the key elements of problem solving.

When you are working on a data science project, these are some of the key parts that you need to work on:

  • Problem Statement

  • Data Acquisition

  • Data Dictionary

  • Stress test 1 - Will review if the framed their work correctly and understand their data correctly and if they are choosing the correct class of models to solve their problem (review step 1)

  • Feature extraction

  • Data Cleaning

  • EDA and Data Visualization

  • Deriving Key insights from EDA

  • Stress test 2 - Review step 4 through 8 to successfully pass the stress test to move to step 9

  • Model building

  • Evaluation

  • Deriving Key Insights from model

  • Stress Test 3 - Review step 9 through 11 

  • Exporting the model

  • Stress Test 4 - Final review if the deployment was successful
