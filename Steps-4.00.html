Phase 1: Project Planning - Colaberry Network Overview

What's the Bigger Picture

  • This documentation process is very tedious.  There are quite a few steps, so I would like to explain the bigger picture so you have a better understanding of the why.  Ideally Colaberry would be able to have enough clients where we can hire directly out of the training program and build teams for clients all over the world.  Students/Consultants would be able to work in an environment they learned from.  It would make the transition from training to employment smooth and we can deploy this model anywhere in the world.   We will get there, but this documentation goes a long way at getting us there.                                                                                                                                                                                       

  • The problem with Data Analytics and Data Science is that not too many people understand it.  Data is going to take over and Company's are still not educated on what they need.  This documentation process was created to educate the World on what's possible with data.  As Colaberry educates the world, they will reach out to us for their data needs and that translates to us hiring people like you.

  • All projects will be available to you and all Alumni/Colaberry Network. 

  •  The best documented projects will be shown to clients.

  •   The best documented projects will be added to our new Marketplace.

  •  The best documented projects will be added to your BIO.

  • In short, the more creative you are in documenting your project, the more impact your project will have on the world.   That's a tall order.  That's why it is important to put the effort into making your project stand out. 

  • Besides the benefits to the outside world, there are many benefits for you in documenting your work.  Here are just a few.

  • Education – Teaching others and using your learning experience to help others learn faster.        

  • BIO – Creating a BIO of all your work will be useful when looking for that next position.       

  • Level Up – You get an extra level of understanding on topics you document. 

  • It is our job to educate the world on Data.  We can not do that unless we are able to document and explain things in such a way that everyone can understand.  This project will be an ongoing, living breathing standard on how to document your projects in Colaberry Network.  Upon completing this project, your work will be documented in a way that syncs with all Colaberry/Refactored platforms.  That will enable us to …

  • Educate the World

  • Contribute to Colaberry Network

  • Build you an online presence

  • Share on Social Media

  • Generate future clients/work for yourself
