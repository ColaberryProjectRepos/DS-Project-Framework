Managing a Project

Daily Updates:

Every day you work on your project, you will comment on your ticket in Basecamp 

  • Which step you are on in the Documentation Process

  • A detailed summary of what you worked on 

  • Screenshot of what you worked on 

Managing Due Dates:

You set your own due dates, but you must ensure that

  • All task have due dates

  • All due dates are in the future. 

  • Before you change the dates, you must add a comment to your project answering these 4 questions.

                         a.  Reason for changing date:

                         b.  Summary of where you are in the project

                         c.  New Due Date

                         d.  Why you think you will complete the project by the new date

If they are late, the Project Manager will start questioning you on your progress.

Make sure you comment on your ticket with an update EVERY DAY you work on it.  It doesn't matter if it was for 5 minutes or 5 hours.   This data will then be pulled in and tracked through app.colaberry.com.  Ensure consistency when documenting and updating the task on your project.  Store all of the details of your project in this task. Besides making it an informative advantage for you, It will also make it easier to document the project upon completion.
