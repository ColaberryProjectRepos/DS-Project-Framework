How to start a Project

  • Every project will be documented.  Not only will you document the project at completion, but you will also document the things you worked on everyday you work on it. 


  • When you finish working on a project and a couple of weeks go by, you will forget all the details of everything you worked on.  That's why it is extremely important to use this process while working throughout your project. 


  • Navigate to your Center of Excellence program based on your Tool. All Projects will be managed in Base Camp and followed up on and reviewed by a Project Manager. (Screenshots of Basecamp will be included) With steps on how to set up.

  • Learn/Review basecamp instructions
